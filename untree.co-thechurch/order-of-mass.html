Order of Mass

Introductory Rites

Entrance Antiphon / Hymn

Priest: In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit.
People: Amen.

Priest: The Lord be with you.
People: And with your spirit.

Penitential Act:

Priest: Brothers and sisters, let us acknowledge our sins…

All: I confess to almighty God... (Full Confiteor)

Priest: May almighty God have mercy on us…
People: Amen.

Kyrie (Lord, Have Mercy):
Priest/People: Lord, have mercy.
People: Lord, have mercy.
Priest/People: Christ, have mercy.
People: Christ, have mercy.
Priest/People: Lord, have mercy.
People: Lord, have mercy.

Gloria: (On Sundays except during Advent and Lent)
All: Glory to God in the highest…

Liturgy of the Word

First Reading

Reader: The Word of the Lord.
People: Thanks be to God.


Responsorial psalm

– (People respond after each verse)


Second Reading

Reader: The Word of the Lord.
People: Thanks be to God.



Gospel Acclamation:

All: Alleluia, Alleluia…

Gospel

Priest: The Lord be with you.
People: And with your spirit.
Priest: A reading from the Holy Gospel according to N.
People: Glory to you, O Lord.

Priest: The Gospel of the Lord.
People: Praise to you, Lord Jesus Christ.

Homily

Profession of Faith (Nicene Creed)
All: I believe in one God, the Father almighty…

Prayer of the Faithful (General Intercessions)
Reader: We pray to the Lord.
People: Lord, hear our prayer.

Liturgy of the Eucharist

Presentation of the Gifts / Offertory Hymn

Priest: Blessed are you, Lord God…
People: Blessed be God forever.

Invitation to Prayer:
Priest: Pray, brethren…
People: May the Lord accept the sacrifice at your hands…

Preface Dialogue:
Priest: The Lord be with you.
People: And with your spirit.
Priest: Lift up your hearts.
People: We lift them up to the Lord.
Priest: Let us give thanks to the Lord our God.
People: It is right and just.

Holy, Holy (Sanctus):
All: Holy, Holy, Holy Lord God of hosts…

Eucharistic Prayer (varies by Sunday)

Memorial Acclamation:
All: We proclaim your Death, O Lord, and profess your Resurrection…

Doxology and Great Amen:
Priest: Through Him, with Him…
People: Amen.

Communion Rite

Lord's Prayer (Our Father)

Sign of Peace
Priest: The peace of the Lord be with you always.
People: And with your spirit.

Lamb of God:
All: Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world…

Communion:
Priest: Behold the Lamb of God…
People: Lord, I am not worthy…

Concluding Rites

Announcements (if any)

Priest: The Lord be with you.
People: And with your spirit.

Final Blessing

Priest: Go forth, the Mass is ended.
People: Thanks be to God.

Recessional Hymn

Mbido (Introductory Rites)

Abụ Mbata

Fada: N’aha Nna, na Nwa, na Mụọ Nsọ.
Ndị Ụka: Amen.

Fada: Udo nke Onye nwe anyị noyere unu n’ebe unu nọ.
Ndị Ụka: Nonyekwara gị

Okwu Nchịkọta Mmehie:

Fada: Ka Chineke ji ike niile mere anyi ebere ma gbaghara anyị njọ anyị...
Ndị Ụka: Amen.

Uwe Ncheta (Kyrie):
Fada: Onyenwe anyị, nwee ebere.
Ndị Ụka: Onyenwe anyị, nwee ebere... (na-aga n’ihu)

Otito Nke Chineke (Gloria):
Ndị Ụka: Otito dịrị Chineke n’eluigwe, udo kwa n’elu ụwa... (na-aga n’ihu)

Mgba Ọgụ Mbụ
Onye na-agụ: Okwu nke Onyenwe anyị.
Ndị Ụka: Daalụ Chineke.

Abụ Azịza Mazị (Na-azaghachi abụ n'etiti amaokwu)

Mgba Ọgụ Nke Abụọ
Onye na-agụ: Okwu nke Onyenwe anyị.
Ndị Ụka: Daalụ Chineke.

Abụ Gospel (Alleluia)
Ndị Ụka: Aleluya, Aleluya...

Oziọma
Fada: Udo diri unu.
Ndị Ụka: Ya no kwa n’ebe gị nọ.
Fada: Oziọma dị nsọ sitere na Matiu/Mak/ etc.
Ndị Ụka: Otito diri gị, Nna.

Fada: Oziọma nke Onyenwe anyị.
Ndị Ụka: Otito diri gị Kraịst.

Ozi (Homily)

Ikekwe Okwukwe:
Ndị Ụka: Ekekwasiri m na Chineke ukwu, Nna Onye kere eluigwe na ụwa...

Ekpere Ndị Ọzọ (Prayer of the Faithful)
Onye na-agụ: Anyị na-arịọ gị, Onyenwe anyị.
Ndị Ụka: Gee ekpere anyị.

Emume Nsọ (Liturgy of the Eucharist)

Ịnye Ọra (Offertory)
Fada: Ngọzi diri gị, Chineke ukwu, n’ihi na...
Ndị Ụka: Ngọzi diri Chineke ukwu rue mgbe ebighị ebi.

Ekpere Nnyefe:
Fada: Kpee ekpere, ka a nabata onyinye anyị…
Ndị Ụka: Ka Onyenwe anyị nabata onyinye n’aka gị…

Mmegide Nsọ:
Fada: Udo diri unu.
Ndị Ụka: Ya no kwa n’ebe gị nọ.
Fada: Lenu anya elu.
Ndị Ụka: Anyi elechago.
Fada: Ka anyi kele Onyenwe anyị.
Ndị Ụka: Ọ dị mma, ziri ezi.

Ndị Ụka: Nsọ, Nsọ, Nsọ, Chineke ukwu, Chineke nke ndị agha...
